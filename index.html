Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love what you wear. If something isn't right, here's how to send it back.

30-Day Return Window

You may return unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to your original payment method.

  • Items must be in original, sellable condition
  • Proof of purchase or order number is required
  • Final sale items are not eligible for return
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of us receiving your return

How to Start a Return

Email jamalbambi21@gmail.com with your order number and the item(s) you'd like to return. We'll send you instructions and a return address. Pack the item securely with your order slip and drop it off at your nearest courier point.

Exchanges

Need a different size? Mention this when you email us and we'll prioritise getting the correct size out to you as soon as your return is received, subject to stock availability.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo and we'll arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

How to Measure

  • Bust/Chest: Measure around the fullest part, keeping the tape level
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, just above the navel
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips

Between sizes? We recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit, which matches our overall design philosophy.
